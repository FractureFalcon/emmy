Welcome to the EMDR-assistance app.
EMDR stands for "Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing".

It is a type of therapy that helps people heal from the symptoms and emotional distress resulting from disturbing life experiences. It can help people with all kinds of negative memories, from the embarassing to the truly traumatic.

If you believe you would benefit from this type of treatment, please click "Begin Treatment."
